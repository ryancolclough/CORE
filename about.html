About StoneCarta

Built on legacy.
Structured for continuity.

StoneCarta began with a direct need to make the governance, bylaws and institutional records of a Masonic organization easier to understand, manage and preserve.

Built from inside the work

CORE and ORE were developed from practical experience with committee responsibilities, meeting preparation, bylaw review, amendments, evidence and corporate continuity.

Carrying purpose forward

The founder’s connection as a descendant of a lodge charter member reinforces the central belief behind StoneCarta: governance is a responsibility inherited from those who built before us and preserved for those who follow.

The larger vision

Jurisdiction-configurable governance infrastructure.

The platform is intended to support not-for-profit organizations across jurisdictions. CORE’s operating engine remains consistent while the annual comparison framework is configured around the law, regulator, parent organization and governing documents applicable to each customer.
