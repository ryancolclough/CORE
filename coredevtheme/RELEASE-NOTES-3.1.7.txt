CORE 3.1.7 — FULL-PAGE COMPLIANCE RECORD
Build 20260718.417

Fixes the iOS/PWA interaction defect where the Reasoning & Draft modal closed when touched or swiped.

Reasoning & Draft now opens as a dedicated, normally scrollable CORE page. It no longer uses the modal layer, so:
- tapping text cannot close the record;
- normal page swiping and browser scrolling work;
- the full record can be read on iPhone;
- Back to Action Centre is always available at the top and bottom.
