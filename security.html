Security and ownership

Your organization’s records.
Your organization’s control.

StoneCarta is designed around organization-controlled records, defined user roles and clear governance responsibility.

Organization-controlled storage

CORE is designed to connect with the organization’s own Google Drive or Google Workspace structure.

Role-based visibility

Officers and committee members receive access based on their authorized responsibilities.

Direct source records

Users can work from connected records rather than copies scattered across personal accounts.

Continuity procedures

Officer transitions can be handled through defined access, record and responsibility handovers.

Data minimization

Only information needed for the organization’s governance workflow should be collected and displayed.

Exportable records

Governance records and completed packages should remain available to the organization in usable formats.

Governance disclaimer

StoneCarta provides structure—not legal certification.

CORE organizes governance and compliance-management workflows. It does not provide legal advice, issue legal opinions or certify compliance with any law, regulation, regulator, parent body or governing authority. Each organization remains responsible for its decisions, approvals, filings, records and legal obligations. Significant amendments and final governing-document packages should be reviewed by qualified counsel in the organization’s jurisdiction.

Privacy

Designed for responsible record handling.

A production privacy policy, data-processing terms, retention schedule and security disclosures should be finalized before public customer onboarding. This Version 1 website does not collect data except information deliberately sent through the demonstration request process.
